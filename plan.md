

# AG Restorations — Premium Architectural Website

## Design System
- **Color palette**: Deep Navy Charcoal (#0F1720), Steel Blue (#1E293B), Electric Teal (#00C2A8), Soft Silver (#E5E7EB), Warm Concrete (#F3F4F6)
- **Fonts**: Bebas Neue/Oswald for bold uppercase headlines, Inter for body text
- **Style**: Dark industrial, clean grid layouts, sharp angles, minimal clutter, architectural precision
- **Animations**: Fade-in reveals, teal accent line transitions, hover elevation on cards, button glow effects, parallax hero

## Pages & Structure

### 1. Landing Page (Home)
- **Hero**: Full-width AI-generated image of modern home with metal roof under dramatic sky, navy gradient overlay, headline "Precision Roofing & Restoration Built to Endure", dual CTA buttons
- **Certifications Bar**: Certified · Licensed · Bonded · Insured with silver separators
- **Services Grid**: Two categories (Roofing Services + Additional Services) with icon cards, hover image reveal, Learn More links
- **Why AG Restorations**: Premium copy about long-term solutions, NJ climate expertise, honest pricing, personalized approach
- **Residential vs Commercial Split**: Side-by-side cinematic sections
- **Service Areas**: All 20 Union County towns listed with subtle NJ map background
- **Before & After Gallery Preview**: Slider-style transformations
- **Final CTA**: Dark navy gradient with "Let's Strengthen Your Home Today"

### 2. Roofing Page
- Hero + detailed description, 5-step process breakdown, materials, benefits, FAQs, CTA

### 3. Commercial Roofing Page
- Same premium structure tailored to commercial flat roofs, single-ply, TPO/EPDM

### 4. Remodeling Page
- Interior/exterior remodeling focus with transformation imagery

### 5. About Page
- Premium storytelling format: mission, core values, local Linden NJ expertise, team image

### 6. Service Areas Page
- All 20 towns with descriptions, map visualization

### 7. Gallery Page
- Masonry grid layout with category filters (Roofing, Commercial, Remodeling, Metal, Power Washing), hover zoom animations

### 8. Contact Page
- Business info, contact form with service dropdown & emergency checkbox, map placeholder

## Navigation & Layout
- Sticky header with AG Restorations logo, nav links, and "Get Free Estimate" CTA button
- Footer with contact info, service links, service areas, and social placeholders
- Mobile: sticky call button, hamburger menu, large tap targets

- All images generated via Lovable AI (hyper-realistic style with dramatic lighting, clean skies, NJ suburban homes, metal roofing close-ups, construction crews, before/after shots)

- Desktop-first with full tablet and mobile optimization
